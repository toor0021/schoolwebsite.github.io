"Educating Students For Success In A Changing World"

Welcome to the official website of Guru Teg Bahadur Public School, Chandigarh. This institution was a dream of founder Chairman Late Sardar Ranjeet Singh Riar with the motto to provide quality education to the underprivileged, deprived and weaker section children of the society. The school made its flying start in the year 1986 on the birth anniversary of Khalsa Panth, April 13th and the institution has risen by leaps and bounds since then. With a mission to build a strong nation of healthy individuals, the institution aims to give to the society citizens who help the country to reach to greater and prosperous heights. The school is situated in Sector 15 of city beautiful, Chandigarh. It comprises of a big building with spacious, airy, CCTV-fitted and well-lighted classrooms from Pre –Nursery to Class X. It is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi.

School Campus

The school is designed to enhance teaching-learning process and we ensure that it becomes a second home for our students. We also believe that good infrastructure is a pre-requisite for quality education. The campus is centrally air-conditioned with technology enabled classrooms and includes essential elements like activity corners, mobile library racks, malleable spaces, outlook express etc. The entire School is fully Wi-Fi connected, promoting anywhere and everywhere learning to the learners. All the classrooms are well equipped with Projectors, Smart boards, e-Beams which augment smart learning.
